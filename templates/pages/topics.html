Themes & Topics

Proposed technical sessions include:

  • Engineering seismology (e.g., soil nonlinearity, ground motion prediction, site effects)
  • Geotechnical earthquake engineering (e.g., ground response, liquefaction, SSI, foundation design, innovations)
  • Seismic design, evaluation and retrofit (e.g., retrofits, performance design, isolation and control, non-structural components)
  • Seismic loss and risk assessment (e.g., resilience, hazard/vulnerability, loss estimation, DRR planning)
  • Monitoring and early warning (e.g., SHM and early warning systems, economic implications)
  • Advanced methods for simulation (e.g., advanced and hybrid simulations)
  • Photogrammetry and visualization (e.g., structure-from-motion, UAV reconnaissance)
  • Lessons learned from post-disaster response and recovery
  • Heritage and traditional structures
  • Social vulnerability and risks
  • Risk reduction policies and practices
  • Other special sessions
