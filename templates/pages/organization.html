Organization

Hosting & Leadership

The conference is hosted by NSET in close collaboration with national and international public, academic, and corporate agencies. The overall collaborative effort is led by NDRRMA.

Committees

Organizing Committee: Leads overall planning, governance, coordination, and inter-institutional collaboration.
Advisory Committee: Provides strategic guidance and support from senior experts and well-wishers.
Technical Committee: Guides technical program quality, tracks, reviews, and session delivery.
