About GeoMandu 2027, 11th AYGEC and 1st SACG

The Nepal Geotechnical Society extends a warm invitation to geotechnical engineers, geoscientists, professionals, and researchers from across the globe to participate in “GeoMandu 2027,” an international conference proudly organized by NGS. NGS has secured the bid from ISSMGE to host the 11th Asian Young Geotechnical Engineers Conference (11th AYGEC), alongside the 1st South Asian Conference on Geotechnics (1st SACG) under Association of Geotechnical Societies of South Asia (AGSSA). The event will take place in Kathmandu from March 17th to 19th, 2027, with the central theme “Mountain Geotechnics for Infrastructure Development.”
