Organizers

The program is organized by the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Authority (NDRRMA), Government of Nepal. It is hosted by the National Society for Earthquake Technology–Nepal (NSET). These organizations work to reduce disaster risks and improve earthquake safety in Nepal. They focus on policy, research, and practical solutions for disaster resilience. Their efforts aim to protect lives, infrastructure, and communities.
